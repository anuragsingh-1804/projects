WORD HUNT

Beat the buzzer-guess the word before your chances vanish!


Guesses left: 6

How to Play

    Start of Game
  • The game displays a word as blanks (e.g., _ _ _ _ _ for a five-letter word).
      • Outcome
  • Winning: Fill in all blanks correctly before chances run out to win.
  • Losing: Failing to guess the word within six incorrect attempts results in a loss; the correct word is then revealed.
      • Guessing Letters
  • Players choose one letter at a time to check its presence in the word.
  • Correct guesses reveal all occurrences of the letter (e.g., if "apple" is the word and "p" is guessed, it appears as _ pp_ _).
  • Incorrect guesses count against the player, reducing their remaining chances.
  • Players have six chances to guess incorrectly.
